Conquest game:
Map: Large Provinces of China (~22) or Large (>3mil) Countries of Europe (~29)
Teams: 4-6
Each team starts with 1 capital and a uniform number of territories
Actions:
-Attack: attack an occupied territory. The owner has 3 minutes to respond – otherwise attacked wins.
-Each team can launch only up to 4 attacks simultaneously. (Total of up to (4-6) x 4 = 16-24 battles at any moment.)
-Defend: respond to an attack. Then both teams get the same question to solve within 10 minutes. Faster response wins – otherwise defender wins.
-Attack capital: must be defeated 3 times. Final time: the fallen kingdom is merged into the victor.
Value:
Starter value:
-Capital 1000 (=100+3x300)
-Territory 300
-Increase after each battle: +100
-(Maybe) consolation price: +50 to the capital
